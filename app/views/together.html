Together event

Our next DDaT Together event will be held at the Kia Oval on Wednesday 24 October at the Kia Oval.

In response to feedback Joanna and the leadership team will provide more information about the DDaT structure and priorities and will be seeking your input on progress to date. We are fortunate to have two keynote speakers on the day: Glyn Williams (DG xxx) in the morning and Sir Philip Rutnam, Home Office Permanent Secretary, in the afternoon.

This event is open to everyone in DDaT but space is limited so places will be allocated on a first-come-first-served basis. You must register via this app to secure your place to attend the event. For those who cannot join in person, DDaT Infrastructure & Platforms will be running another live streaming on the day which will be bookable in advance. Please go to the Register to attend section to book web-streaming.

Don’t forget to bring your Home Office pass. We don’t want you to wear it but you must show it when you arrive at The Kia Oval. If you don’t bring it, you may not be able to take part for security reasons.

Any questions? Please contact the DDaT Communications team and we will get back to you asap.

Wifi details and other information

Registration and refreshments

Registration will take place in the Ashes Suite which is situated on the first floor from 8.30am. If your travel arrangements mean you need to arrive a little early you will be able to work and/or network with other DDaT colleagues in the Pakistan Room on the first floor until registration commences at 8.30am. Our DDaT Together volunteers will be on hand to welcome you and to direct you to the Pakistan Room or the Ashes Suite (both rooms are accessed via the lifts or stairs on your right as you enter the Kia Oval reception).

Please ensure you bring your Home Office pass with you. You will need to show this when you arrive/at registration for security reasons.

Lunch is included in the conference package and will also be served in the Ashes Suite. The Oval will provide sandwiches and seasonal salads which will include vegetarian and vegan options.

Please note: Any dishes known to contain nuts will be identified; however all their dishes may contain nut traces. If you have specific dietary requirements you are welcome to make your own arrangements for lunch on the day if you’d prefer.
